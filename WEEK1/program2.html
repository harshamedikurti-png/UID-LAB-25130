Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, popularly known as Amrita University, is one of India’s leading private deemed universities, renowned for its excellence in academics, research, and societal contribution. Founded by Mata Amritanandamayi Devi, the university is inspired by a strong value-based education system that integrates knowledge with compassion and social responsibility. The university is accredited with the highest A++ grade by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), reflecting its commitment to quality education and institutional excellence. In recognition of its outstanding performance, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham was ranked 7th among universities in India in the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) 2024 rankings. Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham offers more than 300 academic programs across diverse disciplines, including engineering, medicine, sciences, management, arts, and humanities, catering to over 30,000 students from across India and abroad. The university places a strong emphasis on research, innovation, sustainability, and societal impact, addressing real-world challenges through interdisciplinary approaches. Currently, the university has 19 constituent schools spread across ten campuses located in Coimbatore, Amritapuri (Kollam), Kochi, Bengaluru, Amaravati, Chennai, Faridabad, Mysore, Nagercoil, and Haridwar. Each campus is equipped with modern infrastructure and fosters an environment conducive to academic excellence and holistic development.
